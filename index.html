Rules And Hints

Rule of the game

from bicyclecards.com

Play Scoring

  • Count = 15: 2 Points
  • Run of 3 or more: Points are eqaul to run length
  • Pairs: 2pts
  • 3 of a Kind: 6pts
  • 4 of a Kind: 6pts
  • Count = 31: 2pts
  • Go: 1pt
  • Right Jack: 1pt to Dealer

Hand Scoring

  • Cards that sum to 15: 2 Points
  • Run of 3 or more: Points are eqaul to run length
  • Pairs: 2pts EACH
  • Flush: 4 or 5pts
  • Right Jack: 1pts

Useful Hints

  • When scoring your hand you can use the stater card and ALL combinations of cards
  • Runs do not have to be played in order
  • The Right Jack is the Jack of the sit of the starter card
  • The nondealer leads in each phase
  • The Dealer gets to score the crib, and scores the crib last
